Privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

In short

This site offers visual coaching tools. The tools run locally, in your browser: whatever you type or choose stays on your device and is not sent to me or anyone else. You don't need an account, and I don't ask for personal details to use the tools.

Who runs this site

The site is run by Ana-Maria Brad, coach, based in Iași, Romania. For any question about privacy, you can write to me at annamaria.brad@gmail.com.

What data the site collects

  • Your answers in the tools (notes, choices, scores): they stay in your browser. If a tool lets you save or export a PDF, that also happens locally, on your device.
  • Anonymous usage statistics: I use a privacy-friendly analytics service (Umami), with no cookies and nothing that identifies you. I only see general things, like how many people opened a page, never who.

Booking a session

If you choose to book a session, the button takes you to Calendly, an external service. There, the details you fill in (name, email, answers to the questions) are processed by Calendly under their own policy, so that we can arrange a meeting together.

Legal basis and your rights

I process personal data in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Romanian law. Because the tools run locally, in practice I hold no data about you from your use of the site.

You can always ask to see what data I hold about you, to correct it, to delete it, or to withdraw your consent. Just email me. You can also complain to your local data protection authority (in Romania, the ANSPDCP).

Changes

If I update this policy, I'll change the date above. Feel free to check back now and then.
