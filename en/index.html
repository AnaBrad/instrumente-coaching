GROW Model

Four questions from John Whitmore (Goal, Reality, Options, Will) that take you from intention to a concrete first step.

WOOP Model

Gabriele Oettingen's method. Set the outcome you want against the inner obstacle, then build an "if-then" plan. For when you keep putting things off.

WOOP with real obstacles: TikTok, FOMO, "I'm not good enough". You check first whether it's actually your own wish.
