Coaching tools

Visual tools for coaching sessions — 1:1, with teams, or for working on your own.

Structured coaching · action

GROW Model

Four questions from John Whitmore (Goal, Reality, Options, Will) that take you from intention to a concrete first step. Useful at the end of a session or as an assignment between meetings.

Open →
Teenagers · action

GROW Model for teenagers

GROW adapted: questions that separate "what I want" from "what others say", a small first step within 48 hours, and a plan for what comes next.

Open →
Structured coaching · motivation

WOOP Model

Gabriele Oettingen's method. You hold the wish you want next to the inner obstacle, then build an "if-then" plan. Useful when you keep putting things off.

Open →
Teenagers · motivation

WOOP Model for teenagers

WOOP for teenagers, with real obstacles: TikTok, FOMO, "I'm not good enough". You check first whether it's actually your own wish.

Self-discovery · personal values

Personal Values

You choose, drop, and rank, until only the values that truly guide you remain. Useful at the start of a process or before a big decision.

Teenagers · personal values

Personal Values for teenagers

36 values from a teenager's life (authenticity, friendship, freedom, sleep, play, purpose) sorted into 6 areas. You choose, drop, and rank, until the 3 that define you remain.

Teams · diagnosis

GRPI Model

Four pillars in order: Goals, Roles, Processes, Interpersonal relationships. You diagnose top down and see where the blockage really comes from, before jumping to team building. For a coach or team leader.

Adults · control & energy

Circle of Control

After Stephen Covey. You sort what weighs on you into three circles: what you control, what you can influence, and what isn't up to you. You put your energy where it actually works. Useful when you feel overwhelmed.

Three circles for what weighs on you: friends, the group, social media, school. You see what's in your hands and what isn't your fault (bullying included), so you can put your energy where it actually works.
