Discover what really matters to you

This exercise helps you see more clearly what's important to you — not what your parents, teachers or friends say, but what you feel matters.

You'll go through 4 simple steps to find the 3 values that describe you best.

1
You choose 15 values from the full list
2
You narrow them down to the most important 10
3
You pick your top 5
4
You rank them and see your profile

There are no right or wrong answers. Go with your first instinct — choose what you feel you are, not what you think you should be.

10 - 15 minutes. Your progress saves automatically. Your data stays on your device — nothing is sent anywhere.
